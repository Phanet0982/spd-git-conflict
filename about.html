Learning Methodology

The SPD Course utilizes a "Chaos Engineering" approach to learning. By intentionally creating edge-case scenarios like Modify/Delete conflicts, students gain the confidence to handle high-stakes production issues.

"Version control is not about saving code; it is about managing the story of your project's evolution."
